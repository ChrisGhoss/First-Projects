# 🎨 [Paint Cans Counter](/paint-counter/paint_cans_counter.py)
Ever wondered how many cans of paint are needed to paint your room? So did I! Which is why I made a program that wisely calculates the amount needed based on the room's measurements. That same program also includes the quality of the paint: it heavily affects the number of cans needed, since not everyone gets the same paint quality it was necessary to add such option. Whether you paint houses for a living or just want to do it yourself, using this program is a smart choice that can be time effective and somewhat accurate.

## 💻 Code and Logic
The code is made of basic concepts in Python, meaning anyone can understand it with a quick glance.

*What it does:* It asks for measurements, as well as whether you have premium or regular paint cans to get the most accurate amount of cans needed for your room.
